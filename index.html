Todd Warren

Todd Warren is an educator, investor and software professional based in Seattle Washington. In fall of 2020, Todd is teaching at Ashesi University. He also is an adjunct faculty member at Northwestern University where he has taught for more than 10 years. Prior to the focus on teaching, Todd spent more than twenty years at Microsoft in a variety of roles, and most recently was a managing director of Divergent Ventures, a venture capital firm focused on early stage software and internet ventures.

When he is not teaching, advising, or hacking on software himself, Todd is an avid bicyclist; primarily riding a tandem bicycle with his wife. He's also learning to play guitar.
